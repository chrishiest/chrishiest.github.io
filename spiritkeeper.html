Disclaimer: This game was made by 36 random people in a 4 week game jam!

You are a wandering healer who can see and interact with spirits, also called a spiritkeeper.

 As you wander through the peaks and valleys of Vindt you will help villagers troubled by strange illnesses and unexplained phenomena. 

Using your knowledge, you will diagnose ailments, solve problems, and gather elemental essence to strengthen the shrine’s protective veil. 

Spirits are not to be feared, but understood and handled with care.

Play incremental mini-games to gather more essences.
